Education Loan

 Despite the financial shortcomings, Asian Law College motivates the students to accomplish their dreams to pursue higher education. Many candidates studying with us are benefitted after availing education loan through various public/private banks and NBFCs across India.


Asian Law College has tied up with YES Bank and Singh financial services to help the students avail education loan easily.



Procedure for Availing Bank Loan Assistance:
Students seeking for education loan from any bank or NBFC have to apply for a bonafide letter issued by the college. The college will provide the requisite letter and all the supporting documents as required by the bank for loan purposes, as and if believed appropriate. Students have to mail their request for loan letter at both the following email ids:
 

admissions@asiancollegebilari.com
Info@asiancollegebilari.com



Note: The students' parents/guardians shall be responsible for obtaining sanction and repayment of the sanctioned loan and all kinds of liabilities and consequences arising thereof, if any. The college shall not be liable and responsible in this regard.
