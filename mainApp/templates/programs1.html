Bachelor of Law (LLB 3 Years)

 

BA.LL.B.

Asian Law College offers a unique opportunity to study law with a nation-wide academic reputation. The college endeavours towards bringing social, political and economic change with the help of law. It can be achieved only with professionals who are committed to the aforementioned objectives and have a keen interest in the field of law. The faculty members and staff at Asian Law College are well experienced and committed to the study of law. The courses have been designed to cater to a holistic development of students. The College has attempted to create a balance course structure with a fine combination of curricular schedule and co-curricular activities. The various Centres regularly organize competitions and training programs to inculcate professional skills in the students. The College aims towards shaping the students into skilled and hard working professionals.

The College offers BA. LL.B integrated program as a part of its undergraduate program. The Integrated program aims to get lawyers ‘industry-ready’. The integrated degree is a modern approach to legal education. In an integrated course, a student covers two courses under one academic program, one in arts and another in law. In integrated law course, the initial 2 years of study deals with the basic subjects of that course i.e arts and in the last three years legal subjects dominates the curriculum.

Admission to the B.A.LL.B.-5 Year Integrated program is made strictly on merit based on the performance of students in the Asian entrance test 'AET' & personal interview of the student.



To be eligible for admission in the program, the candidate should:

• Have passed Senior School Certificate Examination (10+2 System) or equivalent Examination with at least 45% marks in aggregate for General Category & 40% marks in aggregate for SC/ST Category.

• No Age Limit (MJP Rohilkhand University and Bar Council of India norms).

• Candidates appearing/appeared for the Annual Examination in March/April are also eligible to apply.

Note - All the above mentioned rules & regulation are subject to MJP Rohilkhand University and Bar Council of India.

Documents required at the time of personal interview;
• 10th Mark Sheet/Certificate (Photocopy & original),
• 12th Mark Sheet/Certificate (Photocopy & Original),
• Residence Proof,
• Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS); and
• Six passport size recent photograph.

Intake
• Total Seats: 120 (One hundred and Twenty only).

Admission Process
Admission to BA.LL.B is made through screening at three levels which are as follows:
• Entrance Test
• Personal Interview
• Merit

Duration of the Course and Semester System:
The BA.LL.B Programme is a five year course. Each academic year is divided into two semesters. The odd semesters range from July to December and the even semesters from February to June.
 

Career Opportunities Dress Code Program Content

Career Opportunities

• Criminal Lawyer: Specializes in criminal laws CrPC, IPC, Evidence Act and various other penal laws.

• Civil Litigation Lawyer: Specializes in civil laws e.g. taxation laws, excise laws.

• Legal Analyst: Works for corporate firms or law firms and analyses laws pertaining to the sphere of the company and its operation.

• Document Drafting Lawyer: Specializes in drafting various documents containing agreements, terms and conditions, case material, etc.

• Legal Journalist: Covers crime beats, legal proceedings in courts, arbitration courts, international courts and arbitration events.

• Legal Advisor: Offers consultancy to corporate firms regarding their legal obligations, duties, legal relations with other firms.

• Government Lawyer: Works for the government and in close coordination with the police.

• Judge: Offers judgment after conducting the court proceedings and hearing al the concerned parties.
