Admission Process

BA.LL.B. - 5 Year Program

For admission to BA.LL.B., candidate should have
1. Passed 10+2 with min 60% or Score of National level entrance test viz. CLAT/ LSAT-India / AILET / MHCET / BHU / GGSIPU / JAMIA / AMU etc.
2. Test score of each candidate shall be scaled down to Maximum Marks : 50 / candidate score.
3. Candidates who have scored less than 60% or are appearing in class 12th shall apply for Asian Online Test in order to qualify for the final round of selection process i.e Personal Interview.


LL.B. - 3 Year Program

For admission to LL.B., candidate should have
1. Passed 10+2+3 with minimum 50% or Score of National level entrance Test viz. DU.LL.B. / LSAT / BHU / MHCET etc.
2. Candidates who have scored less than 50% or are appearing in graduation final year shall apply for Asian Online Test in order to qualify for the final round of selection process i.e Personal Interview.

IMPORTANT NOTE:

• Personal Interview is a basic aptitude analysis of students based on the knowledge of subjects studied in the last qualifying exams, other areas of interest, current affairs, hobbies and achievements.
• Any candidate who qualifies Step - 01 has to apply for Step - 02 i.e. Personal interview for the final selection.
• Asian Online Test will be a home-based online test which can be easily accessed through Laptop, Desktop, I-Pad / Tab, Smartphone.
